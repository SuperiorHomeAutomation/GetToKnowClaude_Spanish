## About This Book

*Get to Know Claude* is a practical guide to one of the most powerful thinking partners available to ordinary people today. Written for anyone — not developers, not tech specialists — it takes you from your first conversation with Claude to genuine competence across every area of daily life where a knowledgeable, patient AI partner makes a real difference.

Claude is not a search engine. It is a thinking partner. You bring the ideas, the experience, the vision — Claude brings the knowledge, the patience, and the skill to build on them. Together you produce something neither of you could have created alone.

This book was written with Claude as a collaborator. Every chapter was outlined in conversation, drafted section by section, and refined through honest feedback. The ideas, the stories, and the judgment are the author's. The speed, the language, and the patience to revise belong to Claude.

## About the Author

**Parmod K. Gandhi** has spent fifty years at the intersection of hardware and software. He built his first computers by hand in the 1970s — before the IBM PC existed. He designed industrial automation systems and conducted research in speech recognition and human-machine interaction, earning an honorary appointment as Adjunct System Scientist at Carnegie Mellon University from Dr. Raj Reddy, the pioneer of speech recognition technology.

He created the Buddy Microphone hardware product line — featuring what he believes was the industry's first purely hardware-based noise cancellation system — and is the founder of Superior Home Automation Corp. in Port Colborne, Ontario, Canada.

*Get to Know Claude* is his third book written with Claude as a thinking partner.
